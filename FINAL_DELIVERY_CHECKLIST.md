## 💡 TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### **Engage Participants**
- Pause at Slides 3-4 (challenges/pain) and ask questions
- Have them discuss in pairs: "Which of these do YOU face?"
- Write their pain points on a board
- Refer back to these throughout the course

### **Maintain Motivation**
- Show Slide 6 (Data to Action) multiple times
- Remind: "You're learning to solve these problems"
- Celebrate when they complete realistic exercises
- Use their own industry examples

### **Balance Theory & Practice**
- Follow the 30-60-10 ratio
- Use complete notebooks to show solutions
- Have them practice immediately
- Review exercises before moving on

### **Handle Frustration**
- Data analysis can be frustrating
- Have them look back at Slide 4 (pain points)
- Remind: "This is exactly what we're solving"
- Normalize errors as part of learning
